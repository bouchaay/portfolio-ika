Dream Lashes was born from a personal challenge and a dream. As someone with sensitive skin and allergies, Ika, the founder, struggled to find false lashes that were both comfortable and beautiful. Every attempt to wear false lashes ended in discomfort, and the local market offered little in terms of high-quality, gentle options.

Determined to solve this issue, Ika borrowed money from her brother and set out to create a brand that would change the game. With a fierce entrepreneurial spirit, she launched Dream Lashes, focusing on crafting lashes that not only enhanced natural beauty but also provided unmatched comfort. Her passion and dedication quickly paid off—within a short time, Dream Lashes was thriving, and Ika was able to repay her brother’s loan.

Today, Dream Lashes offers 13 unique styles, each designed to cater to different eye shapes and preferences. From subtle to bold, each pair of lashes is crafted with care, ensuring that every woman can find her perfect fit. At Dream Lashes, we believe that comfort and beauty go hand in hand, and every detail is meticulously considered to make sure your experience is nothing short of perfect.

“Because, after all, every dream deserves to come true”
