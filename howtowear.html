How to Clean

STEP 1


Measure lash strip to eye to check
length and trim band if needed.

STEP 2


Coat top lashes along the lash band
and let sit for about 30 seconds.


STEP 3


Use your fingers to press the false lashes and false
lashes together to create one cohesive lash.

STEP 1


Soak your false lashes in
a warm water bowl with a drop of dish soap for about 5 minutes.

STEP 2


Remove one of the false eyelash from the water bowl and towel dry.
The ones you are not working with, just leave them soaking.


STEP 3


Use your fingers to pull off loose glue residues.


STEP 4


Brush the false eyelashes to remove any excess residue.
Store in original tray and let it air dry.
